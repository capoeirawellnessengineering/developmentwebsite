Collegial Capoeira

Capoeira games may be played collegially or adversarially. Fight is one extreme manifestation of Capoeira. Dance is the other. Ideally, Capoeira is a perfect balance of the dance and fight aspects of this Afro-Brazilian tradition. Embracing the flow of movement in a Capoeira game with equanimity is a serenity practice that leads to this optimal manifestation of the artform.
