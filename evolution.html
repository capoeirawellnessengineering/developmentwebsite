Experience Evolution

Contramestre Zumbi famously founded the Cord√£o de Ouro Capoeira Group in Seoul in 2009, but he and his students there were constantly sore from training Miudinho sequences. The playful and fluid Capoeira game of Miudinho requires a lot of flexibility and strength. Recovering quickly from muscle fatigue was of paramount importance. Zumbi found that finishing workouts with mindfulness of breathing to relaxed the body and led to more rapid recovery. Zumbi also learned that adding the practice of contrast hydrotherapy at the sauna nearby really help to soothe inflamed joints and sore muscles.

Combining these three practices was the genesis of highly-rated, Capoeira Wellness Airbnb Experiences. From those original half-day retreats in 2017, Capoeira Wellness evolved into today's six month course.
